<p><p>The membrane position sensors market is categorized into linear and rotary types. Linear membrane position sensors are designed to measure displacement in a straight line, often used in applications requiring precise linear movement detection. Conversely, rotary membrane position sensors detect angular positions, suitable for applications involving rotational movements. Both types utilize flexible membranes to sense position changes, offering advantages like high sensitivity, durability, and compact design, making them essential in automation, robotics, and control systems across various industries.</p></p>
