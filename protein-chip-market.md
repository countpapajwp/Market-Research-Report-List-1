<p><p>The protein chip market consists of several types, including reverse phase protein microarrays, functional protein microarrays, and analytical microarrays. Reverse phase protein microarrays enable the simultaneous measurement of multiple proteins from complex samples, focusing on their expression levels. Functional protein microarrays assess the functional interactions and activities of proteins, crucial for drug discovery. Analytical microarrays provide quantitative analysis of proteins and biomolecules, enhancing diagnostics and research applications. Each type plays a vital role in advancing proteomics and biotechnology.</p></p>
