<p><p>The Smart Tennis Sensor market comprises two primary types: Bluetooth real-time recording and non-Bluetooth real-time recording. Bluetooth real-time recording sensors connect wirelessly to smartphones or devices, allowing players to receive instant feedback on their performance, such as shot speed and spin. In contrast, non-Bluetooth real-time recording sensors function independently, collecting data without wireless connectivity. Both types enhance player training and performance analysis, but Bluetooth sensors offer a more interactive and connected experience.</p></p>
