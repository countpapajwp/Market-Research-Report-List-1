<p><p>Online invoicing software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based invoicing software operates over the internet, allowing users to access their invoicing data from anywhere, facilitating real-time collaboration and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, offering more control over data security and customization but requiring manual updates and maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs, depending on preferences for accessibility, cost, and control.</p></p>
