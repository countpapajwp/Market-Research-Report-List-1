<p><p>The Fiber Optic Gyrocompass market is categorized into two main types: single-axis and multi-axis fiber optic gyrocompasses. Single-axis fiber optic gyrocompasses are designed to measure rotational movements around one axis, making them suitable for simpler navigation applications. In contrast, multi-axis fiber optic gyrocompasses can detect and measure rotation along multiple axes, offering enhanced precision and stability, making them ideal for advanced navigation systems in aviation, maritime, and other high-performance applications requiring accurate orientation data.</p></p>
