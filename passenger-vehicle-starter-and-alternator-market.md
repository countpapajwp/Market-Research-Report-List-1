<p><p>The passenger vehicle starter and alternator market comprises two key components: the starter motor and the alternator. The starter motor initiates the engine's operation by converting electrical energy into mechanical energy, allowing the vehicle to start. The alternator, on the other hand, generates electrical power to recharge the battery and supply energy to the vehicle’s electrical systems while the engine runs. Together, they ensure reliable vehicle operation and electrical efficiency, making them critical for modern automotive functionality.</p></p>
