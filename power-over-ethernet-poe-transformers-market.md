<p><strong>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flyback Type</li><li>Forward Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) transformers market primarily includes two types: Flyback and Forward transformers. Flyback transformers are commonly used in applications requiring high voltage isolation and multiple output voltages, suitable for low to medium power devices like IP cameras and wireless access points. In contrast, Forward transformers are more efficient for higher power applications, offering less electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for powered devices demanding precise voltage regulation. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient power delivery in networked devices.</p></p>
