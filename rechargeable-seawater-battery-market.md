<p><ul><li>Natural Sea Water Battery</li><li>Blended Sea Water Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rechargeable seawater battery market comprises two main types: natural seawater batteries and blended seawater batteries. Natural seawater batteries utilize raw seawater as an electrolyte, capitalizing on the abundant salts and minerals found in oceans for energy storage. In contrast, blended seawater batteries combine seawater with other materials or additives to enhance performance, efficiency, and energy density. Both types offer environmentally friendly solutions for energy storage, leveraging the vast resources of seawater to meet growing energy demands sustainably.</p></p>
