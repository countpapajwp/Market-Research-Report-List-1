<p><strong>The Trap Primers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Flow</li><li>Pressure Drop Activated</li><li>Flush Valve Operated</li><li>Electronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trap primers are devices that ensure proper water levels in trap seals to prevent foul odors from sewer gases. The continuous flow type maintains a constant water supply, preventing evaporation. Pressure drop activated primers trigger water flow when a pressure drop occurs, replenishing the trap seal as needed. Flush valve operated primers activate during flushing, allowing a timed water release. Electronic trap primers use sensors to monitor trap seal levels, providing a precise and efficient replenishment system.</p></p>
