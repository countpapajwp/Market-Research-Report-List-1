<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) sensors market consists primarily of two types: PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors. PTC sensors increase resistance with rising temperature, making them suitable for high-temperature applications, while NTC sensors decrease resistance as temperature rises, commonly used for precise temperature measurements. These sensors are crucial for monitoring exhaust temperatures in automotive and industrial applications, enhancing engine efficiency and emissions control. Market growth is driven by the rising focus on fuel efficiency and environmental regulations.</p></p>
