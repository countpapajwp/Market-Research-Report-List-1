<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) market includes two primary types of wave sensing: longitudinal wave acoustic sensing and transverse wave acoustic sensing. Longitudinal wave acoustic sensing detects vibrations along the direction of wave travel, ideal for monitoring large infrastructures like pipelines or railway tracks. In contrast, transverse wave acoustic sensing captures vibrations perpendicular to the wave direction, offering insights into subsurface geology and structural integrity. Both types provide essential data for applications in civil engineering, oil and gas, and security monitoring.</p></p>
