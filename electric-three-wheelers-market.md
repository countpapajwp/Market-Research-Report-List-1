<p><p>The electric three-wheeler market comprises two primary types based on motor placement: side wheel motors and center motors. Side wheel motors are positioned on either side of the vehicle, providing enhanced stability and maneuverability, making them ideal for urban commuting. In contrast, center motors are centrally located, offering balanced weight distribution and improved overall performance. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and applications, contributing to the diverse landscape of electric three-wheelers in various markets.</p></p>
