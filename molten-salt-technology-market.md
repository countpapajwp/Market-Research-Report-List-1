<p><p>The molten salt technology market encompasses various solar thermal energy systems including Parabolic Trough Systems, Power Tower Systems, Dish/Engine Systems, and Others. Parabolic Trough Systems utilize curved mirrors to focus sunlight onto a receiver containing molten salt for heat storage. Power Tower Systems deploy tall towers with mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a central receiver. Dish/Engine Systems utilize parabolic dishes to track sunlight and generate power via Stirling engines. "Others" may include hybrid systems or innovative configurations enhancing thermal energy storage and efficiency.</p></p>
