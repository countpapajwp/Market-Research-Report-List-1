<p><ul><li>Kettle Type Reboilers</li><li>Thermosyphon Reboilers</li><li>Fired Reboiler</li><li>Forced Circulation Reboilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reboilers market comprises several types, each catering to specific industrial needs. Kettle type reboilers are efficient for low-pressure applications, providing excellent liquid-vapor separation. Thermosyphon reboilers utilize natural circulation for heat transfer, making them economical and reliable. Fired reboilers rely on direct combustion for vaporization, ideal for high-temperature processes. Lastly, forced circulation reboilers employ pumps to enhance fluid movement, suitable for high-viscosity fluids. Each type plays a crucial role in industrial distillation and heat exchange processes.</p></p>
