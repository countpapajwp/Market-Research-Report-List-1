<p><p>The rehabilitation exoskeleton market is categorized into three main types: lower body, upper body, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons primarily assist individuals with leg mobility issues, aiding in walking and balance. Upper body exoskeletons enhance arm and shoulder mobility, benefiting those recovering from stroke or injury. Full body exoskeletons provide comprehensive support, facilitating overall rehabilitation for patients with severe mobility impairments, allowing for more natural movement and engagement in physical therapy sessions.</p></p>
