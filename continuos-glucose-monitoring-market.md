<p><strong>The Continuos Glucose Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retrospective CGM (r-CGM)</li><li>Real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) market comprises two primary types: retrospective CGM (r-CGM) and real-time continuous glucose monitoring (RT-CGM). r-CGM devices allow users to review glucose data after the fact, aiding in trend analysis and long-term management. In contrast, RT-CGM provides immediate glucose readings and alerts, enabling timely decisions for insulin dosing and diet management. Both types enhance diabetes management but cater to different user preferences and needs in monitoring glucose levels.</p></p>
