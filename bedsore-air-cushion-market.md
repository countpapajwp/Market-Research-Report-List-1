<p><p>The bedsore air cushion market features two main types: automatic inflating air cushions and self-inflating air cushions. Automatic inflating air cushions utilize an electric air pump for quick and efficient inflation, allowing for adjustable pressure and enhanced comfort, particularly for patients at high risk of bedsores. In contrast, self-inflating air cushions rely on built-in foam that expands to fill with air when the valve is opened, providing convenience and portability without the need for an external power source. Both types aim to prevent pressure ulcers and improve patient comfort.</p></p>
