<p><p>Mesocarbon microbeads (MCMB) are critical components in lithium-ion batteries, enhancing performance and energy density. The market for MCMB production can be categorized into several types. Direct thermal condensation involves the rapid conversion of carbon precursors into microbeads through high-temperature processes, yielding high-purity products. The emulsion process, on the other hand, utilizes surfactants to create a uniform mixture, followed by carbonization, which results in well-distributed microbeads. Other methods may include chemical vapor deposition or polymer-based techniques, each offering distinct advantages in manufacturing efficiency and material properties.</p></p>
