<p><p>The Automotive Standalone HVAC market encompasses two primary types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC relies on user inputs for temperature and airflow adjustments, offering a straightforward and cost-effective solution, primarily found in budget-friendly vehicles. In contrast, Automatic HVAC systems utilize sensors and advanced controls to automatically regulate temperature and airflow based on cabin conditions and user preferences, enhancing comfort and efficiency. This technology is prevalent in mid to high-end vehicles, reflecting advancements in automotive climate control.</p></p>
