<p><p>The shochu market is primarily divided into two types: multiply distilled and singly distilled. Multiply distilled shochu undergoes several rounds of distillation, resulting in a cleaner, higher-proof spirit with a lighter flavor profile, making it versatile for cocktails and mixed drinks. In contrast, singly distilled shochu retains more natural flavors from its base ingredients, offering a richer, more complex taste. This type is often enjoyed neat or on the rocks, appealing to those who appreciate traditional craftsmanship.</p></p>
