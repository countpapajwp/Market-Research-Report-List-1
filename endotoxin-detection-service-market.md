<p><p>The endotoxin detection service market comprises three main types: the Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, which utilizes a gel formation process to indicate endotoxin presence; the Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, which employs color changes in a substrate solution for quantification; and the Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test, which measures changes in light transmission caused by bacterial endotoxin interactions. Each method offers unique sensitivity and specificity, catering to various industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology that require rigorous endotoxin testing for safety and compliance.</p></p>
