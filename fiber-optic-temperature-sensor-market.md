<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distributed Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor</li><li>Fiber Optic Fluorescence Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic temperature sensor market primarily includes distributed fiber optic temperature sensors and fiber optic fluorescence temperature sensors. Distributed sensors utilize the entire length of the fiber to monitor temperature changes over large distances, providing real-time data in vast areas. In contrast, fiber optic fluorescence temperature sensors rely on luminescent materials embedded within the fiber, which change their fluorescence properties with temperature variations. Both types are essential for applications requiring high sensitivity, safety, and the ability to operate in harsh environments.</p></p>
