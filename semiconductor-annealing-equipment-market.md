<p><p>Semiconductor annealing equipment is crucial for enhancing the properties of semiconductor materials after fabrication. The market is primarily divided into lamp-based and laser-based types. Lamp-based systems utilize high-intensity lamps to uniformly heat the semiconductor wafers, optimizing crystalline structures and reducing defects. In contrast, laser-based systems employ focused laser beams for precise heating, allowing for rapid processing and minimal thermal damage. Both technologies aim to improve performance and yield in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to different production needs and efficiencies.</p></p>
