<p><p>Thick film resistors are widely utilized in electronic circuits for their reliability and precision. The market is divided into two primary types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Through Hole types. SMD resistors are compact and designed for mounting directly onto the surface of circuit boards, enhancing production efficiency and saving space. In contrast, Through Hole resistors are inserted into drilled holes on a PCB, providing robust physical stability. Both types cater to different applications based on space constraints and assembly methods.</p></p>
