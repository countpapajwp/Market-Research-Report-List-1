<p><p>The refrigeration oil market primarily consists of two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refined petroleum, offers cost-effectiveness and basic lubrication properties, making it suitable for traditional refrigeration systems. In contrast, synthetic oil, formulated from chemically engineered substances, provides superior thermal stability, lower volatility, and enhanced lubrication, benefiting modern high-efficiency refrigeration applications. Both types cater to varying performance requirements and environmental regulations, influencing their adoption across diverse industries and refrigeration technologies.</p></p>
