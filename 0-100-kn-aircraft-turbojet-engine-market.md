<p><strong>The 0 - 100 KN Aircraft Turbojet Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial-Flow Type Turbojets</li><li>Centrifugal Type Turbojets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 0 - 100 KN aircraft turbojet engine market is primarily divided into two types: axial-flow and centrifugal turbojets. Axial-flow turbojets utilize a series of rotating blades to compress air and fuel, resulting in higher efficiency and thrust, making them ideal for larger aircraft and high-speed applications. In contrast, centrifugal turbojets compress air using a spinning impeller, offering simpler construction and better performance at lower speeds, suitable for smaller aircraft or specific operational roles. Both types cater to diverse aviation needs.</p></p>
