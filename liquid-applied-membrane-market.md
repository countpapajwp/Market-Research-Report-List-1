<p><ul><li>Bituminous Membrane</li><li>Elastomeric Membrane</li><li>Cementitious Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Applied Membrane market comprises three main types: Bituminous Membrane, Elastomeric Membrane, and Cementitious Membrane. Bituminous membranes are petroleum-based, offering excellent waterproofing and durability, ideal for roofing applications. Elastomeric membranes are flexible and resilient, accommodating structural movement while providing superior UV resistance. Cementitious membranes, made from cement-based materials, are ideal for concrete surfaces, delivering robust moisture protection and mold resistance. Each type serves distinct applications, ensuring effective waterproofing and long-lasting durability in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
